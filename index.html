Watt

Watt is a technology aimed to build self-sustaining mining around the world that use green energy. Watt goes beyond the idea of conventional cryptocurrency. Our community believes that the future cryptocurrencies will be mined and maintained by using only renewable energy. But Watt is not just about green mining. Our ambition is to create a new eco-system, where Watt token is used in various projects from securing underground water reserves to protection of digital rights technologies and building new hydro-power plants to serve local communities.
